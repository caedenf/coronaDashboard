Coronavirus Dashboard

Symptoms

Common Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Continuous, dry cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Tiredness

Less Common Symptoms

  • Aches and pains
  • Nasal congestion
  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhea

Emergency Symptoms

  • Trouble breathing
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • Confusion
  • Bluish lips or face

Quick facts

  • Some people who become infected with Coronavirus are asymptomatic (do not show symptoms).
  • About 80% of people recover from Coronavirus without needing special treatment.
  • Older individuals and people with medical problems such as high blood pressure, heart problems or diabetes are more likely to develop complications.

Information taken from CDC, NHS, and WHO
