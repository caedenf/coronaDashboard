Coronavirus Dashboard

Symptoms

Common Symptoms

  • Fever
  • Continuous, dry cough
  • Shortness of breath
  • Tiredness

Less Common Symptoms

  • Aches and pains
  • Nasal congestion
  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhea

Emergency Symptoms

  • Trouble breathing
  • Persistent pain or pressure in the chest
  • Confusion
  • Bluish lips or face

Quick facts

  • Coronavirus is spread through contact with droplets expelled by the coughing or exhalation of an infected individual.
  • Older individuals and people with medical problems such as a weakened immune system, high blood pressure, heart disease, lung disease, cancer or diabetes are more likely to develop complications.
  • Some people who become infected with Coronavirus are asymptomatic (do not show symptoms).
  • About 80% of people recover from Coronavirus without needing special treatment.

Information taken from CDC, NHS, and WHO

Tips and Resources

How to Protect Yourself and Others

  • Regularly and thoroughly wash your hands.
  • Stay at least 1 meter away from individuals exhibiting Coronavirus symptoms.
  • Avoid touching your face.
  • Avoid unnecessary travel, and practice social distancing.
  • Cough into your elbow or a tissue.
  • If you believe you are exhibiting symptoms of Coronavirus, self-quarantine if possible for 14 days.
  • If you are sick and must be around other people, then wear a face mask.
